Why Kurt?

This foundation — and this platform — exists because Kurt Storey lived music as a way of giving. He didn't care about status. He cared about supporting artists and musicians who needed help. He didn't wait for permission. He acted — with heart, with hustle, and with humility.

"Kurt's generosity was legendary. He gave his time, talent, and support to those who needed it most."

The platform reflects that ethos. It's not just a website. It's a tool to:

  • Support artists and musicians with dignity
  • Connect people with musical opportunities
  • Make sure no one gets forgotten
  • Track every decision without red tape

This page — /why — reminds us why the rest exists.

Kurt's Story

Kurt Storey was a talented sound engineer, musician, and mentor who believed that music could change lives. Throughout his career at Nashville's Station Inn and on tours with renowned artists, he helped countless musicians sound their best and reach their potential.

His approach was simple but profound: support artists with his technical expertise, connect musicians with opportunities, and let the music speak for itself. Kurt never sought recognition for his contributions — he simply loved helping people become better and sharing the joy of music with others.
