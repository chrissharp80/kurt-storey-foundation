Our Mission

The Kurt Storey Foundation exists to support musicians and artists through mentorship, technical assistance, and community building, carrying forward Kurt's passion for music and his dedication to helping others develop their craft.

Kurt's technical expertise was matched only by his generosity of spirit. As a respected sound engineer at Nashville's Station Inn and on tours with renowned artists, he helped countless musicians sound their best and reach their potential. His approach combined technical mastery with genuine human connection.
