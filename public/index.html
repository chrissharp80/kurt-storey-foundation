Our Mission

The Kurt Storey Foundation exists to provide musical instruments to children who cannot afford them, carrying forward Kurt's passion for music and giving.

Kurt never talked about access. He just handed someone a guitar and said, "Play." His approach was simple but profound: remove barriers, provide opportunities, and let the music do the rest.
