Play It Forward

Instruments. Empathy. Legacy.

Our Mission

We believe every child deserves the chance to discover their voice through music. The Play It Forward Foundation exists to provide instruments, mentorship, and support to young people who can’t access them otherwise — because music opens doors, builds confidence, and connects hearts.

This work is inspired by the quiet legacy of Kurt Storey — a sound engineer, friend, and mentor who spent his life helping others shine. He never asked for attention, but gave everything he had to those around him. In his spirit, we carry this mission forward — with empathy, humility, and joy.

“He never made things about himself — ever. Just helped people, taught through presence, and made the room better without needing credit. Who doesn’t fall in love with that?”

What We Do

  • 🎻 Provide free musical instruments to children who cannot afford them
  • 🧠 Connect youth with mentors who nurture creative growth
  • 🎙️ Offer support in live sound and production for those interested in tech
  • 🌱 Focus on underserved communities, especially those with limited access to arts education
